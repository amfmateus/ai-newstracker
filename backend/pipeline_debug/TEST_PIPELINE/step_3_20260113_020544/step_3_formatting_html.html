Labor Market and Wage Adjustments
The Colombian labor market is experiencing significant transformations at the start of 2026, primarily driven by a 23% increase in the minimum wage 1 . This substantial rise is triggering a domino effect of wage compression and internal adjustments throughout salary structures 1 . Experts note that this adjustment reconfigures payroll costs, potentially presenting difficult decisions for both companies and workers concerning formal employment sustainability 1 .
