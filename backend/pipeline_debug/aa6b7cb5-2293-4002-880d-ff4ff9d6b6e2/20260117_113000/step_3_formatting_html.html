Political and Governance Developments
A significant political debate is underway concerning President Gustavo Petro's economic emergency decree, with governors from at least 17 departments voicing opposition and questioning its legal viability 1 , 7 . This "rebellion" highlights concerns over territorial autonomy and the decree's potential impact on departmental finances 8 . The political landscape is further complicated by former President Álvaro Uribe's criticism of President Petro's upcoming meeting with Donald Trump, labeling Petro and Cepeda as "allies of Maduro" 9 . In other governance news, the Prosecutor's Office has launched an investigation into a multi-million peso contract awarded by the Presidency to a firm advising President Petro in the U.S. 10 . Meanwhile, the Senate President, Lidio García, expressed concerns that proposed budget cuts to Congress could jeopardize its functioning 11 . The ELN's continued attacks, including a drone strike resulting in soldier fatalities in Catatumbo, underscore ongoing security challenges 12 , 13 . The expansion of illegal armed groups also poses a risk to upcoming elections 14 . The UNP dismissed a contractor amid sexual harassment allegations, prompting an institutional review 15 .

Economic and Financial Sector Updates
The Colombian financial sector demonstrated robust performance by November 2025, with total profits reaching BCOP 122.08 trillion, an 8.06% increase from the previous year 4 . Bancolombia, Banco Davivienda, and Banco de Bogotá emerged as the top-performing banks in terms of profitability 16 . However, small businesses are facing significant pressure at the start of 2026 due to a 23% increase in the minimum wage and rising surcharges 5 . A legal challenge has been filed against the government's decree to increase the minimum wage by 23%, alleging that legal criteria were not followed 17 . In trade policy, the elimination of tariffs on U.S. powdered milk, as stipulated by the Free Trade Agreement, is set to reach 0% by 2026, raising concerns for domestic producers 6 . The Ministry of Transport is updating the SICE-TAC system with new cost variables, including minimum wage and fuel prices, to better reflect freight transport expenses 18 , 19 . Certificates of Deposit (CDTs) are experiencing significant growth in popularity and investment volume among Colombians 20 , 21 . The government plans to raise approximately $2.5 trillion through increased VAT on tobacco and spirits, a move opposed by 20 out of 32 governors 22 . A significant investment of over US$1.7 billion is planned for electrical works in the Caribbean region to strengthen infrastructure and integrate renewable energy sources 23 , 24 .

Security and Armed Conflict
The Catatumbo region continues to grapple with a prolonged humanitarian crisis, marked by a year of intense confrontations between the ELN and dissident Farc groups, resulting in significant casualties and displacement 25 , . An ELN drone attack in Tibú claimed the life of one soldier and injured four others, highlighting the ongoing threat in the Catatumbo region 12 , 13 . President Petro condemned the ELN's actions, drawing attention to the deaths of young Colombians and Venezuelans at the hands of the group 26 . The expansion of illegal armed groups poses a significant risk to the upcoming elections 14 . Furthermore, U.S. warnings about "military activity" in Colombian airspace have led airlines to activate protocols, although no immediate impact on air navigation has been reported 2 , 3 . The FAA has issued a specific safety alert for the Colombian Pacific region due to potential risks from military activities and GNSS interference 3 . The Army reported an incident where troops were surrounded by civilians during an operation in Nariño, which was resolved through dialogue 27 . Concerns have also been raised about the use of the National Army's budget in Catatumbo for non-essential items amidst the ongoing crisis 28 .

Sector-Specific Developments
In the healthcare sector, the National Superintendency of Health has extended a precautionary measure against Fomag due to persistent care failures, requiring an updated work plan 29 . Similarly, the Attorney General's Office has alerted Nueva EPS about issues with its contingency plan for drug delivery 30 . Nueva EPS has applied for over $1.5 trillion to pay healthcare providers in January 2026 31 . The State Council has ruled that EPS can use UPC funds to pay old debts under specific conditions 32 . In the transportation sector, toll rates have increased across 80 major roads, with some exceeding inflation rates 33 . The Coffee Axis tolls remain in place due to a binding arbitration ruling, despite President Petro's intention to dismantle them 34 . The Ministry of Transport is updating the SICE-TAC system with new cost variables for cargo transport 18 . In sports, Colombian football clubs are offering season tickets at prices up to $1.8 million as a financial strategy amid league challenges 35 . The construction guild warns of legal and access risks associated with a decree aiming to de-index affordable housing 36 .
