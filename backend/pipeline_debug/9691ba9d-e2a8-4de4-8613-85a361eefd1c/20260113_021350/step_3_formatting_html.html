Labor Market Dynamics and Reforms
The Colombian labor market is undergoing significant shifts in 2026, largely driven by a substantial 23% increase in the minimum wage. This sharp rise is creating a domino effect of wage compression and internal adjustments throughout the salary structure, presenting difficult decisions for businesses and potentially impacting formal employment sustainability 2 . Furthermore, the implemented Labor Reform introduces changes to the payment of severance interest, altering how employees and companies manage periodic income and financial planning 3 . These reforms signal a move towards new contractual modalities designed to facilitate income management and financial foresight.
